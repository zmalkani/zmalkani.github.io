Hey! My name is Zach Malkani. I'm a Grade 12 student and prefect at Royal St. George's College. I am super passionate about engineering and rowing - and hope to take both those lives with me into university next year. Since I was a little guy, I've always loved making things. Whether it be multi-floor lego boats to test in the kitchen sink when I was 7, or making sketchy wooden ramps to bike on with my friends in grade 8, I've always been drawn to creating. By Grade 10 when I started the RSGC ACES program, I began to gain knowledge on electronics, and my passion became truly enabled. Since then, I've been making all sorts of things, the best of which are just below here! Thanks for taking a look here and if you'd like to chat feel free to send me an email at zmalkani@rsgc.on.ca.
